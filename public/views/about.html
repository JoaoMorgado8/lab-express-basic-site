It refers to a mountain pass or any of the narrow, winding roads that

geographically similar areas, like the legendary Nordschleife in Germany.
In order to decrease the incline of mountain roads, thereby making
them easier for commercial trucks to pass, engineers place a series of

motorsport enthusiasts because they provide a challenging, albeit
dangerous, course.
